Two devices having IP addresses : 10.1.1.1 and 10.1.2.1 will not be in the same IP addresses if their subnet mask is 255.255.255.0 because the first 24 bits for both the devices does not match up. 

But if their Subnet Mask is 255.255.0.0, then we see that the first 16 bits matches each other, and hence the two devices will be in the same subnet mask.
